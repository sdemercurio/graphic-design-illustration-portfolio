about

Hi! I'm Sarah.

I design things for print and web and have been known to kick out an illustration or two.

                 For most of my life my medium of choice has been pencil or paint, but lately I have been opting
                        for html and css. 
                        
                        I am proficient in Adobe Illustrator, PhotoShop, and InDesign with working
                        knowledge of After Effects. When designing for web, I am comfortable with HTML5, CSS3, some JavaScript, React,
                        NodeJS, and MongoDB, and I'll be sure to put together a prototype in Figma before we start working.

                        When I'm not designing something, you can catch me outside enjoying whatever nature I can get to in the Metro Detroit area, cooking, or taking an hour to watch a cool documentary. I guess the
                        pandemic has kind of limited my extracurricular activities! Most importantly, I'm learning new
                        technologies and doing my best to stay on top of design trends to constantly sharpen my craft.
